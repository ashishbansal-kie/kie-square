01 Inventory Optimization

Inventory Optimization :

Analyzing Demand Patterns at SKU & Regional Level: Use historical sales, seasonality, and external drivers to forecast demand accurately across product lines and geographies. Implementing Dynamic Safety Stock Models: Move beyond fixed buffer stock. Use variability in lead time and consumption rates to set intelligent safety stock levels. Adopting Just-in-Time (JIT) & Reorder Automation: Integrate real-time inventory visibility with supplier response systems to reduce holding costs and enable agile replenishment. Leveraging Predictive Analytics & IoT Sensors : Use AI/ML models and IoT-driven insights from shopfloor and warehouse data to anticipate demand shifts and prevent overstocking/understocking. Aligning Inventory with Production & Sales Strategy: Synchronize procurement, production, and dispatch cycles with real-time sales data to ensure optimized flow across the value chain. Conducting Continuous ABC-XYZ Classification : Regularly segment inventory based on value and volatility to focus planning efforts where business impact is highest. Enabling Cross-Functional Inventory Governance : Form a task force across SCM, finance, and operations to periodically review inventory health and adjust policies collaboratively.

03 Preventive Asset Management

Preventive Asset Management :

Establish Predictive Maintenance Protocols: Use IoT sensors and machine data to monitor asset health in real-time and schedule maintenance before failure signs escalate. Automate Maintenance Scheduling: Deploy CMMS (Computerized Maintenance Management Systems) to streamline inspection cycles, service logs, and alert mechanisms. Analyze Failure Trends & Root Causes: Leverage AI/ML models on historical maintenance data to identify recurring issues, predict risk-prone components, and take targeted action. Integrate Asset Lifecycle Insights : Link maintenance data with asset procurement, utilization, and disposal to enhance capital planning and extend equipment life. PAM is a key practice for organizations seeking to maximize the performance and longevity of their assets while minimizing disruptions and expenses.

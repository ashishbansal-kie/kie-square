Meta 360

In today's fast-paced e-commerce landscape, platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, Nykaa, and Blinkit dominate the market. With thousands of SKUs, evolving algorithms, and aggressive competition, brands need more than just performance tracking—they need deep market insights to stay ahead.

Meta is a cutting-edge e-commerce analytics tool that helps brands monitor, analyze, and adapt by offering real-time competitive intelligence and trend analysis across platforms.

Why Choose Meta?

Meta enables brands to:

  • Track & compare performance across multiple e-commerce platforms.
  • Analyze market trends to stay ahead of competitors.
  • Optimize product listings & campaigns for better visibility and ROI.
  • Monitor share-of-voice (SOV) & brand presence for strategic growth.
Key Features
  • Cross-Platform Intelligence (CPI) - A unified dashboard consolidating data across Amazon, Flipkart, Nykaa, and more.
  • Category Command (CC) - Deep insights into category performance and emerging trends.
  • Product Performance Precision (P3) - Detailed product-level analytics with alerts on underperforming and trending SKUs.
  • Competitive Radar (CR) - Real-time competitor analysis to refine pricing and marketing strategies.
  • SOV Boost (SB) - Tracks organic and sponsored share-of-voice to maximize campaign ROI.
  • Dynamic Trend Analytics (DTA) - AI-powered trend detection to identify key growth drivers.
  • Unified Brand Presence (UBP) - A complete view of online brand consistency across platforms.
Benefits from Meta
  • E-Commerce Managers - Gain real-time insights to optimize sales strategies.
  • Marketing Teams - Improve campaign performance and competitive positioning.
  • Brand Managers - Monitor brand visibility and share-of-voice across platforms.

Plantation Drive

Plantation Drive


  • At KiE Square, we are committed to the longevity and sustainability of our planet.

  • We aim to augment our green footprint by embarking on a Plantation drive, wherein we plant a fruit tree for each KiE team member on their birthday.

  • We hope that we can do our bit for Mother Earth alongside providing nutrient-rich food for the coming generation.
Festival Celebration

Festival Celebration


  • KiE Square believes in the spirit of togetherness and joy.

  • We strengthen our bonds by celebrating festivals and milestones together, through fun activities, food, games, and shared laughter.

  • We create unforgettable memories and express gratitude by fostering a sense of belongingness among all the team members.



Team Building

Team Building


  • At KiE Square, we understand the importance of strong team-building.

  • Our team-building experiences are opportunities for personal growth and building lasting connections.

Birthday Celebration
